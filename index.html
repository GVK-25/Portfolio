Every project here used industry-standard tools to solve genuine engineering challenges — from electronics thermal management to pipeline structural integrity.

Thermal Analysis · ANSYS01

PCM-Integrated Mini-Channel Heat Sink for Electronics Cooling

ANSYS numerical thermal analysis of a mini-channel heat sink integrated with Phase Change Material, designed for high-heat-flux electronics cooling. Simulated under steady-state and transient conditions — PCM integration demonstrated measurably superior thermal regulation over conventional heat sinks, with direct applicability in aerospace avionics cooling.

ANSYSPCM Modelling Transient ThermalHeat Sink Design
Structural FEA · Abaqus02

Plastic Collapse Load of Shape-Imperfect Pipe Bends

Nonlinear FEA in Abaqus to evaluate structural safety of pipeline bends with through-wall circumferential cracks. Parametric study across crack angle, bend angle, ovality, wall thickness, and bend radius — proving that combined geometric imperfections drastically elevate collapse risk in high-pressure industrial piping systems.

AbaqusNonlinear FEA Fracture MechanicsPipeline Safety
Nanofluid · CAE03

Nanofluid Cooling Study under Transient TEC Operations

Comparative thermal analysis of Water–7% Cu nanofluid cooling under rapid TEC fluctuations. Identified trade-offs between fast cooling response and smooth thermal behaviour caused by nanoparticle settling — providing quantitative criteria for practical nanofluid selection beyond simple thermal conductivity benchmarks.

Thermal SimulationTEC Analysis Nanoparticle Dynamics
Gas Systems · MATLAB04

Vent Release Rate & Natural Gas Dew Point — IEC Standard

MATLAB implementation of IEC 60079-10-1 to compute vent release rates (mass flow rate and duration) for industrial gas systems. Complemented by dew point estimation to identify natural gas condensation thresholds — preventing liquid accumulation and supporting compliant, safe pipeline design.
